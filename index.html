Widget Integration

The simplest and easiest way to get integrated.

The simplest and easiest way to get integrated with PrrrfectFit is to use our widget integration. It works in the same way as a facebook like, simple copy a snippet of code onto your site and we’ll take care of the rest. All you need to do is send us the affiliate window product id. If the user doesn’t have an account with PrrrfectFit we’ll sign them up and get their measurements, then we can provide recommendations for PrrrfectFit sizes helping you reduce your return rates.

Want to see our technology?You view it on our websitePrrrfectFit.com, or you can contact us to find out more here.

Rest API Integration

Query us to find the users perfect fit.

Using our REST API you can query us to find out what a users PrrrfectFit is. All you need to do is provide us with an affiliate window product id and the PrrrfectFit user id and we’ll take care of the rest. It’s down to you what you show the user and how you link the user to their PrrrfectFit account. This allows you to keep full control of what’s been shown on your site, whilst still having all the benefits PrrrfectFit taking care of customer measurements.

Want to see our technology?You view it on our websitePrrrfectFit.com, or you can contact us to find out more here.

White Label API Integration

Retain full control of your users experience.

If you want to retain full control we do offer a white label solution for clients. It’ll be down to you to provide us with the users measurements and the product id. Then we can calculate what the PrrrfectFit is for that product. This provides you with the most flexibility, the user won’t even know that you’re using PrrrfectFit to match their sizes.

Want to see our technology?You view it on our websitePrrrfectFit.com, or you can contact us to find out more here.

Affiliate Window data matching

Data matching for easy integration.

In addition to providing great integration options we can make life even easier for you. We can provide you with data matching services allowing you to provide us with your own internal product ids. We’ll match the products on our end, all you have to do is tell the user what size to buy.
